Sponsors

Focus is honored to have the support and collaboration of some of the most prestigious brands in the world of photography and design. Here are some of them:

Canon

It is one of the leading companies in the image sector, with a wide range of products that includes cameras, lenses, printers, scanners and projectors. Canon is characterized by its quality, innovation and reliability, and by its commitment to the environment and social responsibility. Canon provides us with the photographic material we use in our courses, and offers us exclusive discounts and promotions for our students.

Adobe

It is the company that created some of the most popular and used programs in the field of graphic design, video editing and web creation, such as Photoshop, Lightroom, Illustrator, Premiere and Dreamweaver. Adobe stands out for its creativity, versatility and professionalism, and for its commitment to education and culture. Adobe provides us with the editing and retouching software we use in our courses, and offers us free licenses and tutorials for our students.

National Geographic

It is one of the most recognized and respected organizations in the field of exploration, science and conservation of the planet, with a history of more than 130 years. National Geographic is famous for its reports, documentaries and photographs of great quality and informative value, and for its work in raising awareness about environmental and social problems. National Geographic provides us with the content and inspiration we use in our courses, and offers us scholarships and contests for our students.

Thanks to these sponsors, we can offer our students excellent training and a unique experience in the world of photography. We are very grateful for their trust and generosity.
