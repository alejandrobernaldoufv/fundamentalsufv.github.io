About us

The creator of the company

Focus is a company that was established by Alberto Ruiz, who has been passionate, about photography since childhood. Albertos journey began with an analog camera gifted to him by his grandfather and since he has relentlessly pursued learning and experimenting with techniques and styles. He pursued Audiovisual Communication at the Complutense University of Madrid followed by specialization in Artistic Photography at the School of Art 10. Throughout his career Alberto has worked as a photographer for magazines, agencies and brands. His captivating work has been showcased in galleries and museums. Motivated by a desire to share his passion and expertise with others Alberto created Fotocursos—a platform offering high quality photography courses, for all skill levels.

The objective of the company

At Focus our main goal is to teach photography in an enjoyable way tailored to each students needs. We strongly believe that photography is an art that can only be truly learned through hands on experience. That's why our courses are designed to allow students to start practicing with their cameras and devices right, from the beginning. In addition to hands on practice we also prioritize making our courses entertaining and motivating. We incorporate exercises, challenges, games. Provide constructive feedback to keep students engaged and inspired throughout their learning journey. Furthermore we understand that every student has preferences and requirements when it comes to learning photography. That's why we offer a variety of levels, themes and schedules to cater to needs. Our ultimate aim is for each student to not enjoy their experience, with us but also learn and enhance their photography skills along the way. Join us at Focus as we embark on an adventure of capturing moments through the lens!
